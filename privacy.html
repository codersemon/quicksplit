Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device. Always.

App: QuickSplit: Tip & Bill
Developer: Devs Wizard (devswizard.com)
Contact: devswizard@gmail.com
Last updated: June 2025

QuickSplit is designed to be private by default. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This policy explains exactly what that means.

Information We Collect

None. QuickSplit does not collect any personal data. You do not create an account, and you never log in. We have no servers that receive your information.

No Account Required

The app works fully without any sign-up, email, or login. There is no user profile to manage and nothing tying the app to your identity.

Your Calculation History

Any calculations you save are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone, is never uploaded, and is deleted permanently if you clear the history or uninstall the app.

No Data Transmitted

QuickSplit is a 100% offline app for its core features. Your bill amounts, tip settings, and saved history are never sent to any server or third party.

Location Data

QuickSplit does not request, access, or collect your location at any time.

Advertising

QuickSplit is free to use. The app does not currently show ads. In the future we may display ads through Google AdMob to keep the app free. If and when ads are enabled, AdMob may collect certain device information (such as device identifiers and usage data) to serve them. This processing is handled by Google, not by us. You can review how Google uses this data in the Google Privacy Policy and learn more about ads in the AdMob documentation.

Children's Privacy

QuickSplit does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since we collect no personal data at all, the app is safe for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
