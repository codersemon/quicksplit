FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is QuickSplit free?

Yes. QuickSplit is completely free to download and use on Google Play. There is no subscription, no paywall, and no ads that interrupt your calculation.

Does QuickSplit work offline?

Yes. QuickSplit works 100% offline. No internet connection is ever required to calculate tips or split bills — all math runs locally on your device.

How many people can I split a bill between?

You can split any bill evenly among up to 100 people in a single tap.

Do I need an account to use QuickSplit?

No. QuickSplit requires no sign-up, email, or login. There is no account to manage and nothing tying the app to your identity.

Which currencies does QuickSplit support?

QuickSplit lets you switch between USD, GBP, AUD, CAD and EUR currency symbols.

Is QuickSplit available on iPhone?

QuickSplit is currently available on Android via Google Play. It is built with Flutter by Devs Wizard.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data stays on your device. QuickSplit collects no personal data, has no tracking, and never uploads your bill amounts or saved history to any server.
